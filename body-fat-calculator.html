FAQs

How accurate is body fat estimation? BMI method gives estimate. For exact %, use DEXA scan or calipers.

What is healthy body fat %? Men: 10-20%, Women: 20-30% is considered healthy range.

Does age affect body fat %? Yes, body fat % naturally increases with age even at same weight.
