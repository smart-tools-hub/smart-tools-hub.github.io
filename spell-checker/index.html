Top 8 Spelling Mistakes Detected by Our AI

Our dictionary is updated to catch the most frequent errors in modern English writing.

1. Confused Words: Affect/Effect, Your/You're, Its/It's.

2. Doubled Letters: Misspelling words like 'occasion' as 'ocassion'.

3. Keyboard Slips: 'Teh' instead of 'The', 'Manger' instead of 'Manager'.

4. Joined Words: Using 'alot' instead of 'a lot'.

5. Silent Letters: Forgetting letters in words like 'subtle' or 'debris'.

6. Suffix Problems: Writing 'definately' instead of 'definitely'.

7. Prefix Errors: Incorrect use of 'un', 're', 'mis'.

8. American vs British: We currently use US English as the standard.
