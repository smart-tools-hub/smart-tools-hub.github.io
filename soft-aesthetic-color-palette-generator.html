What Is a Muted Pastel Palette?

A muted pastel palette uses desaturated, soft colors that feel calm and modern. Unlike bright colors, these tones reduce eye strain and work great for minimal websites, digital planners, and social media layouts.

Why Designers Prefer Soft Colors

Soft colors make text easier to read and give a premium feel. Brands like Notion, Linear, and Cal.com use muted palettes for this reason. This tool generates those exact tones automatically without you guessing hex codes.

How to Use This Tool

Click "Make New Palette" until you find a combo you like. Copy the hex codes and use them in Figma, Canva, Shopify, or your website CSS. Save 3-4 palettes for your brand kit so your designs stay consistent across platforms.
