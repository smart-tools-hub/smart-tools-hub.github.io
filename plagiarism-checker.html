What is Considered Plagiarism?

Our tool helps you identify different types of plagiarism that can hurt your website and academic career.

Direct Copying: Copying text word by word from another source without citation.
Self Plagiarism: Reusing your own previously published content.
Paraphrasing: Changing a few words but keeping the same structure and idea.
Accidental Plagiarism: Forgetting to cite sources or using common phrases incorrectly.
