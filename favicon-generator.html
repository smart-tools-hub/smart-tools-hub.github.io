What Is Favicon and Why Do You Need It For Your Website?

Favicon is a 16x16 or 32x32 pixel icon that represents your website. You see it in browser tabs, bookmarks, and mobile home screens.

3 Main Benefits:

1. Brand Identity: Users recognize your brand instantly among multiple tabs.

2. Professionalism: 90% of top websites have custom favicon. No favicon = spammy look.

3. Better UX: Helps users find your website quickly in bookmarks.

Google also considers user experience for ranking. A proper favicon improves click-through rate.

How To Add Favicon To Your Website After Download?

For HTML Website:

For WordPress:
Go to Appearance > Customize > Site Identity > Site Icon > Upload

For Blogger:
Theme > Customize > Favicon > Upload
