FAQs

How do you calculate ideal weight? We use BMI range 18.5-24.9 to calculate min and max healthy weight for your height.

Is ideal weight different for men and women? BMI formula is same, but body composition differs. This gives general healthy range.

Should I aim for lowest ideal weight? Aim for middle of range. Focus on muscle mass and body fat, not just weight.
