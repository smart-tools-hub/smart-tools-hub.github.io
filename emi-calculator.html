What is EMI Calculator?

EMI Calculator helps you find exact monthly installment amount for any loan. EMI means Equated Monthly Installment which is fixed amount paid to lender every month. This tool is useful before applying for home loan, car loan or personal loan. You can compare different interest rates and loan tenures to choose lowest EMI option. Banks use same formula to calculate EMI. Our calculator gives instant results without registration. Mobile friendly and works on all devices. Perfect for financial planning and budget management before taking any loan.

EMI Formula Explained Simply

EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1]
P = Principal loan amount
R = Monthly interest rate = Annual rate / 12 / 100
N = Total months = Years x 12
This formula is used by all banks and financial institutions worldwide.

EMI FAQs

What is difference between EMI and interest?
EMI includes both principal and interest. In starting months, EMI has more interest and less principal. In last months, EMI has more principal and less interest. Total EMI remains same every month.

How to reduce EMI amount?
You can reduce EMI by 3 ways: 1) Increase down payment 2) Choose longer loan tenure 3) Select loan with lower interest rate. Use this calculator to test different combinations.

Does EMI change every month?
No, EMI remains fixed for entire loan tenure in fixed rate loans. Only in floating rate loans EMI may change if interest rate changes as per RBI guidelines.
