Top 5 Common Mistakes Our Tool Fixes Instantly

Our basic checker is trained to find the most common errors that Non-Native English speakers make. Here are the top 5 issues we correct:

1. Capitalization at the Start: The tool will automatically add a capital letter to the beginning of your sentence. Example: kamir is a beautiful city becomes Kamir is a beautiful city.
2. Missing Full Stop or Question Mark: It adds the correct ending punctuation. Example: how are you becomes How are you?
3. Incorrect Use of 'i': It changes the small 'i' to a capital 'I' when it refers to yourself. Example: i am fine becomes I am fine.
4. Missing Commas: It helps add commas where needed for clarity. Example: hello how are you becomes Hello, how are you?
5. Extra Spaces: It helps you identify sentences with too many spaces between words.
