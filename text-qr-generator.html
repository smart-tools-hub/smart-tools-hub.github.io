Text QR Code Generator

Convert any text message to a QR code instantly. Share notes, instructions, or contact details with USA customers via QR scan.

Uses for Text QR Codes

USA teachers use text QR codes to share homework links. Stores use them for discount codes. No internet needed to read text.

FAQ - Text QR Codes

How much text can I put in QR code?

Up to 500 characters work well. Longer text creates denser QR code that may be harder to scan.

Can I use this for vCard contact info?

Yes. Paste vCard format text and it will create a contact QR code for phones.

Do I need internet to scan text QR?

No. Text QR codes display the message offline. Only URL QR codes need internet.
