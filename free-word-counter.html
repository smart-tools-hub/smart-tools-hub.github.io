About This Text Counter

This tool is built for bloggers, copywriters, students, and SEO experts who need to check text stats quickly. It works directly in your browser, so your content stays private and loads instantly.

What Makes It Different

Reading Time: Get an estimate of how long it takes to read your text based on 200 words per minute. Useful for blog posts and articles.

Line Count: Count lines for code snippets, poetry, and formatted text.

Live Updates: All stats update as you type. No submit button needed.

100% Free: No signup, no limits, no ads in the tool area.

Common Use Cases

  • Check tweet, Instagram, and LinkedIn post length
  • Prepare assignments and essays for school
  • Analyze SEO meta titles and descriptions
  • Count lines in code and config files
  • Track writing progress for novels and blogs

How It Works

JavaScript runs locally in your browser to analyze the text. Words are split by spaces, lines by line breaks, and reading time is calculated at 200 words per minute - the average adult reading speed.
