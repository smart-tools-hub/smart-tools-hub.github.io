USA Mortgage FAQs

What is current mortgage rate in USA 2026?
Mortgage rates in USA change daily. As of 2026, 30 year fixed rate ranges 6.5% to 7.5%. Check with local US banks for exact rate. This calculator lets you test different rates to plan budget.

How much down payment needed for USA home loan?
FHA loan needs only 3.5% down payment. Conventional loan needs 20% down payment to avoid PMI. Use calculator to see EMI difference with different down payment amounts.
