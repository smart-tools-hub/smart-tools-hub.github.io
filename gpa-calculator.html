How to Calculate GPA Manually

To calculate GPA manually: Multiply each course’s grade point by its credit hours. Add all results. Divide by total credit hours. For example: Course 1: 3 credits × 4.0 = 12. Course 2: 2 credits × 3.0 = 6. Total = 18 ÷ 5 credits = 3.6 GPA.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between GPA and CGPA?

GPA is for one semester. CGPA is cumulative - it averages all semesters together. Our calculator handles both.

What grading scale does this use?

Standard 4.0 scale: A = 4.0, A- = 3.7, B+ = 3.3, B = 3.0, C+ = 2.3, C = 2.0, F = 0.0. You can also enter numbers directly.

Can I calculate CGPA for my whole degree?

Yes. Just add all courses from all semesters with their credit hours and grades, then click calculate.

Does this work for Pakistani universities?

Yes. Most Pakistani universities use 4.0 scale. If yours uses 5.0 or percentage, convert to 4.0 first or enter GPA points directly.

Is my data saved?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your grades are not stored or uploaded anywhere.
