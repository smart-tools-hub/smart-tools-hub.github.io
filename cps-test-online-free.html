What is CPS Test and Why It Matters

A CPS Test measures your clicks per second - how fast you can click a mouse button or tap a screen within a set time limit. CPS stands for Clicks Per Second and it is a popular metric among gamers, especially Minecraft PvP players, who need fast clicking to gain an advantage in combat. But CPS testing is not just for gamers. Typists, data entry workers, and anyone looking to improve their hand-eye coordination can use this free online CPS test to track their clicking speed over time.

Our CPS Test tool lets you choose between 5 seconds, 10 seconds, or 20 seconds mode. The shorter tests measure your burst speed, while the 20 second test shows your sustained clicking ability. Many people start at 6-8 CPS and can train themselves to reach 10-12 CPS with practice. Professional Minecraft players often hit 14-16 CPS using techniques like jitter clicking or butterfly clicking.

Tips to Improve Your Click Speed Score

If you want to increase your CPS, start with proper posture and relax your hand. Tension slows you down. Try using your index and middle finger together on the mouse button for butterfly clicking. Another technique is jitter clicking, where you vibrate your arm to make the mouse click rapidly. Practice daily for 5 minutes and you will see improvement within a week.
