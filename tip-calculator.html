Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate tip without a calculator?

Move the decimal one place left for 10%, then multiply. For 20%, double the 10% amount. Or use this tool for instant accuracy.

What’s the difference between 15%, 18%, and 20% tip?

15% is standard for good service, 18% for great service, 20%+ for excellent service in the USA.

Can I use this for delivery tips too?

Yes. It works for any bill where you need to add a tip - restaurants, delivery, taxis, and more.
