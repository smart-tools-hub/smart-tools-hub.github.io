What is Kids BMI Calculator?

This Kids BMI Calculator calculates Body Mass Index for children and teens aged 2-19 years. Unlike adult BMI, Kids BMI is compared to other kids of the same age and gender using CDC growth charts. It shows where a child stands in their age group. To check adult BMI, use our BMI Calculator.

How to Use It:

Step 1: Enter height in centimeters
Step 2: Enter weight in kilograms
Step 3: Enter age between 2-19 years
Step 4: Select Boy or Girl
Step 5: Click "Calculate Kids BMI" button

Understanding the Result:

Below 5th Percentile: Underweight. The child weighs less than average. Consult a doctor.

5th to 85th Percentile: Healthy Weight. This is the ideal range.

85th to 95th Percentile: Overweight. Weight is higher than average. Focus on diet and activity.

Above 95th Percentile: Obese. Medical consultation is recommended. You can also check Body Fat Calculator for more details.

Important Note:

BMI is only a screening tool. It does not measure muscle, bone density, or body composition. If the result is high or low, consult a pediatrician. To find a healthy weight range, you can also check the Ideal Weight Calculator.

FAQs

Q1: Is kids BMI different from adult BMI?
A: Yes, kids BMI uses percentiles compared to same age/gender group.

Q2: What BMI percentile is healthy for kids?
A: 5th to 85th percentile is healthy. Below 5th underweight, above 85th overweight.

Q3: Should I worry if my child BMI is high?
A: Consult pediatrician. BMI is screening tool, not diagnosis.
