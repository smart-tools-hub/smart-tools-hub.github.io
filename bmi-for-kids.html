Kids BMI Calculator

Calculate BMI percentile for children and teens aged 2-19. Kids BMI is compared to other kids of same age and gender using CDC growth charts.

What is Kids BMI Calculator?

This Kids BMI Calculator calculates Body Mass Index percentile for children and teens aged 2-19 years. Unlike adult BMI, Kids BMI is compared to other kids of the same age and gender using CDC growth charts. It shows where a child stands in their age group. To check adult BMI, use our BMI Calculator.

How to Use It:

  1. Enter height in cm
  2. Enter weight in kg
  3. Enter age between 2-19 years
  4. Select Boy or Girl
  5. Click "Calculate Kids BMI" button

Understanding the Result:

Below 5th Percentile: Underweight. The child weighs less than average. Consult a doctor.

5th to 85th Percentile: Healthy Weight. This is the ideal range.

85th to 95th Percentile: Overweight. Weight is higher than average. Focus on diet and activity.

Above 95th Percentile: Obese. Medical consultation is recommended. You can also check Body Fat Calculator for more details.

Example:

If a 10-year-old boy with height 140 cm and weight 40 kg, his BMI percentile will be around 85th, which falls in the Overweight category.

Important Note:

BMI is only a screening tool. It does not measure muscle, bone density, or body composition. If the result is high or low, consult a pediatrician. To find a healthy weight range, you can also check the Ideal Weight Calculator.

FAQs

Is kids BMI different from adult BMI? Yes, kids BMI uses percentiles compared to same age/gender group.

What BMI percentile is healthy for kids? 5th to 85th percentile is healthy. Below 5th underweight, above 85th overweight.

Should I worry if my child BMI is high? Consult pediatrician. BMI is screening tool, not diagnosis.
